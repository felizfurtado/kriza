Frequently Asked Questions

Here’s what most couples ask us (you might be wondering the same!)

How early should we book our event?
Ideally, 9-12 months in advance for weddings — especially during peak season (Nov–Feb). But we also pull off stunning events in shorter timelines. Don’t hesitate to reach out!
Do you plan destination weddings?
100%! Goa and Karwar are our home turf, but we've styled events across India. We also offer remote planning for couples living abroad or in other cities.
Can I customize a package?
Absolutely. All packages are flexible. Add what you love, skip what you don’t. We’ll create a personalized quote after our initial chat.
Which locations do you serve?
We’re based in Goa and Karwar, but love working in Bangalore, Mumbai, and all over India. Need help somewhere else? Let’s chat about travel options!
