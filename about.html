OUR STORY

Decor That Speaks the Language of Love

FromHeaven Events by Kriza was born out of a deep love for celebration and styling. Based in Karwar and Goa, our journey began with a simple mission — to make every event feel magical, elegant, and full of heart.

Over the years, we've designed countless engagements, weddings, and receptions, always customizing every detail to reflect each couple’s uniqueness and traditions.

Whether it's a beach wedding in Goa or a cozy ceremony in Karwar, our team pours creativity and soul into every decor element.

What makes FromHeaven Events special is our commitment to perfection and personal touch. From the first flower to the final goodbye, every moment is handled with love.

We work with trusted vendors, bring fresh design ideas, and help you stay stress-free while we handle the magic behind the scenes.

Currently booking for 2025 and 2026, we’re here to bring your vision to life — with grace, detail, and beauty.
