OUR STORY

Weaving Dreams Since 2010

Founded in the heart of Mumbai, Lovio began as a small boutique wedding planning service with a vision to create celebrations that are as unique as the couples we serve. What started as a passion project has blossomed into one of India and Europe's most sought-after wedding planning companies.

Our philosophy is simple: every love story is unique, and every wedding should reflect that individuality. We don't believe in cookie-cutter celebrations. Instead, we work closely with each couple to design an event that authentically represents their personalities, values, and dreams.

Over the past decade, we've had the privilege of orchestrating over 300 weddings across three continents, each one more beautiful than the last. Our team of dedicated professionals brings together expertise in design, logistics, and hospitality to create seamless, stress-free wedding experiences.

What sets Lovio apart is our meticulous attention to detail and our commitment to excellence. From the initial consultation to the final farewell, we handle every aspect of your wedding with care and precision.

We pride ourselves on our extensive network of trusted vendors, our creative design solutions, and our ability to anticipate needs before they arise. Whether you're planning an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, we have the expertise to make your vision a reality.

Based in Mumbai with satellite offices in Paris and Florence, we specialize in destination weddings that showcase the beauty of each location while maintaining the cultural traditions that are important to you and your family.
