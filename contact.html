Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to the most common questions about our wedding and event planning services.

How far in advance should I book your services?
Ideally, we recommend booking 6–12 months in advance for weddings—especially during the busy season. For smaller events, 3–6 months is usually perfect. But even if it’s last-minute, reach out! We might still have availability.
Do you handle destination weddings outside of Goa?
Absolutely! We love planning destination weddings all over India—and even abroad. From Rajasthan to Kerala to dreamy international locations, we’ll bring your vision to life wherever you are.
What is included in your wedding planning packages?
Our packages are flexible and tailored to your needs. They range from full-service planning to partial coordination and always include vendor management, timeline creation, and day-of coordination. Full-service packages also include design, budgeting, and unlimited consultations.
Can you work within my budget?
Yes, absolutely. We’re pros at creating magic within all kinds of budgets. During our first consultation, we’ll understand your vision and help you make the most of your resources—without compromising on style or experience.
Do you offer floral and decor services?
Definitely! Floral and décor are at the heart of what we do. We design everything—from custom floral arrangements to stunning installations—to match your theme and aesthetic beautifully.
